A product mindset, technical skills, and a go-to-market strategy

These are the three crucial components every tech entrepreneur needs to master. Over the span of 14 weeks, the Adelson School of Entrepreneurship in Interdisciplinary Center Herzliya (IDC) and Le Wagon Coding Bootcamp will bring together experienced mentors, teachers, investors, like-minded entrepreneurs and the immersive experience of Startup Nation to build your MVP and learn how to launch a startup.
